accountabubble

hackathon project | Jan 2020 - present
React
Team: Megan Calverley, Ash Shah, Claire Beard

Inspired by the Women in Informatics 9th Annual Hackathon's theme 'Hacking Forward, Together', accountabubble is a productivity tracker built for groups of people to work toward goals together. Members of a 'bubble' see progress on their individual tasks reflected in the context of the entire team. Whether your bubble consists of your coworkers, classmates, or friends, accountabubble makes keeping each other on track simple and low stress.

Check out our prototype website's design and implementation. Post hackathon, we plan on implementing a mobile version - explore our working design here.
