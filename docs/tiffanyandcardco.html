tiffany & card co.

personal project | June 2020 - present
Next.js, Vercel

My sister is the talent behind the handmade card account, @tiffanyandcardco. I noticed she was using Instagram as her portfolio and to take requests from people. However, Instagram isn't necessarily the easiest or most accessible platform for everyone to use. In an effort to make viewing and requesting her work easier, I created a simple website for her using Next.js and deployed using Vercel.
