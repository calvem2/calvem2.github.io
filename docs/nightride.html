Night Ride

INFO 200: Foundations of Informatics | Dec 2018
Adobe Xd

For this project, we were challenged to design or improve a technology in order to benefit a target group. I chose to focus on the safety of UW students who utilize the school's Night Ride shuttle service. I designed a mobile application that would allow the user to track shuttles, get directions, and view transportation schedules.

Check out the high fidelity design and the overview of the final product.
